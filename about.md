I am Assistant Professor of the Flight Dynamics department of the Instituto Tecnológico de Aeronáutica ([ITA](http://www.ita.br)), in São José dos Campos, Brazil. 

I have a PhD degree (2016) in Control from the Université de Toulouse - [ISAE](http://www.isae.fr), France.
 Prior to the PhD, I have a Master of Science degree (2012) in Flight Dynamics and Control from  ITA, and an Aeronautical Engineering degree (2008) from the same institute.

My main research interests are [fluid-structures interactions modeling and control]({{ site.baseurl }}phd), [flexible airplanes flight dynamics and aeroelasticity]({{ site.baseurl }}flexible_airplanes)
and [port-Hamiltonians systems]({{ site.baseurl }}port-hamiltonian).
