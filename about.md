Since January 2013, I am a PhD student at [ISAE](http://www.isae.fr), in Toulouse, France.
I have a scholarship from the [CNPq](htttp://www.cnpq.br) (Brazilian Council for Scientific
and Technological Development).


Prior to the PhD, I have a Master of Science degree (2012) in Flight Dynamics and Control from [ITA](http://www.ita.br) (Aeronautics Institute
of Technology), in São José dos Campos, Brazil, and an Aeronautical Engineering degree (2008) from 
the same institute.

Since 2012, I am Assistant Professor at ITA. 

My main research interests are [fluid-structures interactions modeling and control]({{ site.baseurl }}phd), [flexible airplanes flight dynamics and aeroelasticity]({{ site.baseurl }}flexible_airplanes)
and [port-Hamiltonians systems]({{ site.baseurl }}port-hamiltonian).
